Arduino Hardware and Programming: Making a clock.

Having been introduced to Arduinos during the first year of my Software engineering degree at Aberystwyth University I decided I really wanted to try and persue different projects with my Arduino. Therefore after finding and purchasing a hardware package for the Arduino I decided to create my own clock using the LCD screen provided and a serial clock circuit.
